About Aerotype

Student-led aerospace innovation at the forefront of autonomous UAV technology. We're engineering the future of unmanned aerial systems through collaborative research and cutting-edge development.

Our Mission

To advance the field of autonomous aerospace systems through innovative research, collaborative engineering, and practical application of cutting-edge technologies. We believe that the future of aviation lies in intelligent, coordinated unmanned systems that can operate safely and efficiently in any environment.

As a student-led organization, we're committed to bridging the gap between academic research and real-world aerospace challenges, preparing the next generation of engineers to lead the industry forward.

Future Vision

Looking ahead, Aerotype aims to become a leading force in autonomous aerospace systems, transitioning from a student-led initiative to a recognized industry innovator. We envision our technologies enabling new capabilities in search and rescue, environmental monitoring, and infrastructure inspection.

Our roadmap includes expanding our team, establishing industry partnerships, and commercializing our most promising technologies. We're committed to maintaining our student-led roots while building a sustainable organization that continues to push the boundaries of aerospace innovation.

2025 Goals

  • Scale UAV fleet to 100+ units
  • Establish industry partnerships
  • Launch pilot programs with emergency services
  • Pursue commercial licensing opportunities
